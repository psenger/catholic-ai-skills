# Saint Sixtus II (d. 258) — Pope 257–258

### Saint Sixtus II (d. 258) — Pope 257–258

**Birth name:** unknown | **Feast day:** August 7 | **Age at death:** unknown
**Canonization status:** Saint and martyr
**Known for:** martyred during the Valerianic persecution; named in the Canon of the Mass

Sixtus was arrested while celebrating the liturgy in the catacombs of Praetextatus and beheaded on the spot, along with four of his deacons. His deacon Lawrence followed him in martyrdom four days later. Sixtus's name is in the Roman Canon among the early Roman martyrs.
