# Saint Gianna Beretta Molla (1922 – 1962)

### Saint Gianna Beretta Molla (1922 – 1962)

**Feast day:** April 28 | **Age at death:** 39
**Canonization status:** Saint (canonized 2004 by John Paul II)
**Known for:** Italian pediatrician and mother who chose to give birth to her fourth child rather than accept treatment that would have saved her own life

Gianna Beretta was a Milanese pediatrician, married to Pietro Molla, with three young children. In her fourth pregnancy a uterine tumor was discovered. She refused the recommended hysterectomy (which would have ended the pregnancy) in favor of surgery that preserved the child. She told her doctors: "If you have to choose between me and the baby, choose the baby; don't hesitate." Her daughter Gianna Emanuela was born on April 21, 1962. Gianna died a week later from septic peritonitis. Her husband Pietro and her daughter Gianna Emanuela (who became a doctor herself) were both present at her canonization in 2004 — one of the first times a saint's living spouse and child have attended.

**Sources:** Vatican.va canonization homily
