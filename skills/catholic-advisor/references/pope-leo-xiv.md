# Leo XIV (b. 1955) — Pope 2025–present

### Leo XIV (b. 1955) — Pope 2025–present

**Birth name:** Robert Francis Prevost | **Birthplace:** Chicago, Illinois, USA (September 14, 1955)
**Canonization status:** reigning pope
**Reign:** elected May 8, 2025; inaugural Mass May 18, 2025
**Known for:** the first pope born in the United States; an Augustinian friar (Prior General of the Order of Saint Augustine, 2001–2013); a missionary in Peru for many years; Bishop of Chiclayo (Peru), 2014/2015–2023; Prefect of the Dicastery for Bishops (2023–2025) under Pope Francis

Robert Francis Prevost was born in Chicago to a family of Spanish, Italian, and Creole heritage. He entered the Order of Saint Augustine, professed solemn vows in 1981, and was ordained priest in 1982 in Rome. He served as a missionary in Peru, became a Peruvian citizen, and was named Bishop of Chiclayo by Pope Francis in 2014/2015. In 2023 he was called to Rome as Prefect of the Dicastery for Bishops and was made a cardinal. He was elected pope on May 8, 2025, on the second day of the conclave following Pope Francis's death. His chosen name — Leo, the first time it has been taken since Leo XIII (1903) — signals a deliberate commitment to the tradition of Catholic social teaching that *Rerum Novarum* (1891) inaugurated. His first apostolic exhortation, *Dilexi Te*, was issued on October 4, 2025 (the feast of Saint Francis of Assisi).
