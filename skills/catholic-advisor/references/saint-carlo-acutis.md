# Blessed Carlo Acutis (1991 – 2006)

### Blessed Carlo Acutis (1991 – 2006)

**Feast day:** October 12 | **Age at death:** 15
**Canonization status:** Saint (canonized April 27, 2025, by Pope Leo XIV after Francis's death — the first millennial saint and the first saint of the Internet generation)
**Known for:** Milanese teenager who built a website cataloging the Eucharistic miracles of the Catholic Church; daily communicant; died of fulminant leukemia at fifteen, offering his suffering for the pope and for the souls in purgatory

Carlo was a normal Milan teenager who coded websites, played video games, loved soccer, and went to Mass every day. He used his programming skill to build a website cataloging Eucharistic miracles worldwide — a project that has since been translated into many languages and continues to be expanded by the Church. When diagnosed with fulminant leukemia at fifteen, he said: "I offer all the suffering I will have to suffer for the Lord, the pope, and the Church." He died within days, on October 12, 2006. His body, in jeans and a tracksuit, is enshrined in Assisi, where his tomb is one of the most visited modern Catholic shrines. He was beatified in 2020 and canonized by Pope Leo XIV in 2025.

**Sources:** Vatican.va beatification and canonization documents
