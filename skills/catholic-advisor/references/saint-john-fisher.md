# Saint John Fisher (1469 – 1535)

### Saint John Fisher (1469 – 1535)

**Feast day:** June 22 (with Thomas More) | **Age at death:** 65
**Canonization status:** Saint (canonized 1935)
**Known for:** bishop of Rochester; cardinal; the only English bishop to refuse the Oath of Supremacy under Henry VIII; beheaded two weeks before Thomas More

John Fisher had been Henry VIII's tutor and confessor; he was the king's mother's executor. He was a learned humanist and a tireless reformer of the diocesan clergy. He alone among the English bishops refused to take the Oath of Supremacy. Pope Paul III made him cardinal in 1535 while he was already imprisoned in the Tower — Henry responded that he would send the cardinal's head to receive the hat. Fisher was beheaded on June 22, 1535; Thomas More followed him a fortnight later. They share a feast day.
