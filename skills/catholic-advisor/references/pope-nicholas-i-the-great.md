# Saint Nicholas I "the Great" (c. 800 – 867) — Pope 858–867

### Saint Nicholas I "the Great" (c. 800 – 867) — Pope 858–867

**Feast day:** November 13 | **Age at death:** approximately 67
**Canonization status:** Saint
**Known for:** vigorous defense of papal authority against royal interference and against the Patriarch of Constantinople, Photius

Nicholas confronted the Frankish king Lothair II over the king's attempt to divorce his lawful wife and marry his mistress, and the Eastern emperor and patriarch over the deposition of Patriarch Ignatius and the elevation of Photius. He is sometimes counted with Leo I and Gregory I as the "three Great Popes" of the early Middle Ages.
