# Saint Maria Mazzarello (1837 – 1881)

### Saint Maria Mazzarello (1837 – 1881)

**Feast day:** May 13 | **Age at death:** 44
**Canonization status:** Saint (canonized 1951)
**Known for:** co-founder, with John Bosco, of the Daughters of Mary Help of Christians (the Salesian Sisters, 1872)

Maria was a peasant woman of Mornese in Piedmont who, after suffering typhoid in early adulthood and losing her capacity for hard farm work, took up sewing and gathered the village girls for catechesis. Don Bosco met her in 1864 and recognized in her community the female counterpart to his Salesians. The Daughters of Mary Help of Christians were founded in 1872 with Maria as the first Superior General. The community grew rapidly and is now one of the largest women's congregations in the Church.
