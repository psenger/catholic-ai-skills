# Pius IV (1499 – 1565) — Pope 1559–1565

### Pius IV (1499 – 1565) — Pope 1559–1565

**Birth name:** Giovanni Angelo Medici | **Age at death:** approximately 66
**Canonization status:** not canonized
**Known for:** concluded the Council of Trent (1563) and promulgated its decrees; issued the Profession of Faith of Trent (1564)

Pius IV reconvened the suspended Council of Trent in 1562 and brought it to its conclusion the following year. His promulgation of the Tridentine decrees and his issuance of the *Professio fidei Tridentina* — required of clergy and converts for centuries — institutionalized the Tridentine reform.
