# Saint John of Ávila (1499 – 1569)

### Saint John of Ávila (1499 – 1569)

**Feast day:** May 10 | **Age at death:** 70
**Doctor of the Church:** Yes (proclaimed 2012 by Benedict XVI)
**Known for:** the apostle of Andalusia; spiritual director of Teresa of Ávila, John of the Cross, John of God, Francis Borgia, Peter of Alcantara, and others; the *Audi Filia* (a treatise on the spiritual life)

John of Ávila was a diocesan priest of Spain, a great preacher, and a spiritual director of extraordinary breadth. He was the unseen formator of the Spanish mystical tradition — many of its great figures were shaped by his direction. He was canonized in 1970 and named a Doctor of the Church by Benedict XVI in 2012.

**Sources:** Vatican.va apostolic letter declaring him Doctor (2012)
