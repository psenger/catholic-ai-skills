# Sylvester II (c. 946 – 1003) — Pope 999–1003

### Sylvester II (c. 946 – 1003) — Pope 999–1003

**Birth name:** Gerbert of Aurillac | **Age at death:** approximately 57
**Canonization status:** not canonized
**Known for:** the first French pope; one of the greatest scholars of his age (he introduced Arabic numerals and the abacus to Europe and wrote on astronomy and mathematics)

Sylvester took the name Sylvester in conscious imitation of Sylvester I, the pope of Constantine's time, signaling a renewed engagement with empire (now the Holy Roman Empire of Otto III). His brilliance gave rise to legends that he had made a pact with the devil — a slander; he was a man of vast learning and personal piety.
