# Saint Linus (d. c. 76) — Pope c. 67–c. 76

### Saint Linus (d. c. 76) — Pope c. 67–c. 76

**Birth name:** Linus | **Feast day:** September 23 | **Age at death:** unknown
**Canonization status:** Saint
**Known for:** second pope; named in 2 Timothy 4:21 as one of those greeting Timothy from Rome

Linus is named by Irenaeus (Against Heresies III.3) as the immediate successor of Peter, and his name appears in the Canon of the Mass (the Roman Canon, Eucharistic Prayer I) among the Roman martyrs. He is venerated as a martyr though the historical details of his death are not preserved.
