# Saint Stanislaus Kostka (1550 – 1568)

### Saint Stanislaus Kostka (1550 – 1568)

**Feast day:** November 13 | **Age at death:** 17
**Canonization status:** Saint (canonized 1726)
**Known for:** Polish noble; ran away from home at sixteen to join the Jesuits in Rome despite his family's opposition; died less than a year after entering the novitiate

Stanislaus was the son of a Polish senator and from boyhood drawn to religious life. When his older brother and tutor opposed his vocation, he walked 350 miles from Vienna to join the Jesuits at Rome. He was admitted by Francis Borgia, then Superior General, and died nine months later at seventeen.
