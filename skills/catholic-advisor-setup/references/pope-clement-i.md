# Saint Clement I (d. c. 99) — Pope c. 88–c. 99

### Saint Clement I (d. c. 99) — Pope c. 88–c. 99

**Birth name:** Clement | **Feast day:** November 23 | **Age at death:** unknown
**Canonization status:** Saint
**Known for:** fourth pope; author of 1 Clement to the Corinthians (the earliest Christian writing outside the New Testament)

See `church-fathers.md` for a fuller treatment of Clement as Apostolic Father. As pope, his intervention in the affairs of the Corinthian church is the earliest dated external evidence of the Roman bishop's claim to exercise authority beyond the city of Rome itself. Tradition holds he was exiled to the Crimea and martyred by being thrown into the sea tied to an anchor; the historical core cannot be verified.
