# Saint Agatha (d. c. 251)

### Saint Agatha (d. c. 251)

**Feast day:** February 5 | **Age at death:** approximately 20
**Canonization status:** Saint (pre-congregation)
**Known for:** martyred at Catania in Sicily under Decius; in iconography, depicted holding her severed breasts on a platter (her tortures included the cutting off of her breasts); patroness of Catania, of breast-cancer patients, and of wet-nurses

The historical core of Agatha's martyrdom (her death at Catania under the Decian persecution) is attested by the fifth century. The dramatic details, including her healing visions of Saint Peter in prison, belong to later hagiography. Her name is in the Roman Canon.
