# Saint Peter Canisius (1521 – 1597)

### Saint Peter Canisius (1521 – 1597)

**Feast day:** December 21 | **Age at death:** 76
**Doctor of the Church:** Yes (proclaimed 1925 by Pius XI)
**Known for:** the second apostle of Germany (after Boniface); Jesuit catechist of the Counter-Reformation; three catechisms (a Small, Smaller, and Larger Catechism) that shaped German Catholic formation for centuries

Canisius was Dutch by birth, joined the Society of Jesus, and devoted his life to the re-Catholicization of central Europe in the aftermath of the Reformation. His catechisms were lucid, biblical, and tied to controversies of the day; for two centuries "Canisius" was a noun in Germany meaning "the catechism."
