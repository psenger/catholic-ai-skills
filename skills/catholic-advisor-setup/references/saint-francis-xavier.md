# Saint Francis Xavier (1506 – 1552)

### Saint Francis Xavier (1506 – 1552)

**Feast day:** December 3 | **Age at death:** 46
**Canonization status:** Saint (canonized 1622)
**Known for:** the apostle of the Indies and Japan; one of the original seven Jesuits with Ignatius; co-patron of the missions (with Thérèse of Lisieux)

Francis Xavier joined Ignatius at Paris and was one of the seven who took vows together at Montmartre in 1534. In 1541 he was sent to the East: India, then the Moluccas, then Japan (where he spent two and a half years and founded the first Christian community), and finally — attempting to enter China — he died on the island of Shangchuan within sight of the Chinese coast, age forty-six. The number of converts attributed to him runs into the tens of thousands. His incorrupt body is venerated at the Basilica of Bom Jesus in Goa.
