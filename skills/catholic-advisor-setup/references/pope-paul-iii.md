# Paul III (1468 – 1549) — Pope 1534–1549

### Paul III (1468 – 1549) — Pope 1534–1549

**Birth name:** Alessandro Farnese | **Age at death:** approximately 81
**Canonization status:** not canonized
**Known for:** convened the Council of Trent (1545); approved the Society of Jesus (1540); commissioned Michelangelo's *Last Judgment* in the Sistine Chapel

Paul III was the first pope of the Counter-Reformation. He convened the Council of Trent in 1545, after a delay of years, and lived to see it produce its first dogmatic decrees on Scripture, original sin, and justification. He approved Ignatius of Loyola's Society of Jesus in 1540 — the order that would become the principal missionary and educational force of the Counter-Reformation.
