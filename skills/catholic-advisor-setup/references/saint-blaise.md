# Saint Blaise (d. c. 316)

### Saint Blaise (d. c. 316)

**Feast day:** February 3 | **Age at death:** unknown
**Canonization status:** Saint (pre-congregation)
**Known for:** bishop of Sebaste in Armenia, martyred under Licinius; patron of those suffering from diseases of the throat; the blessing of throats on his feast day (with two crossed candles) is a beloved popular devotion

The historical core — a bishop of Sebaste, martyred in the early fourth century — is attested. The legend of his having saved a child from choking on a fish bone (the basis of the throat-blessing) is late and devotional. The throat-blessing remains a vivid annual contact with the saint for many Catholics: "Through the intercession of Saint Blaise, bishop and martyr, may God deliver you from every disease of the throat and from every other illness."
